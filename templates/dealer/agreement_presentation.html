General Dealer Agreement for B2B Website

This General Dealer Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of [DATE] by and between [Your Company Name], a [Your State] corporation with a principal place of business at [Your Address] ("Company"), and [Dealer Name], a [Dealer Legal Entity (Corporation, LLC, etc.)] with a principal place of business at [Dealer Address] ("Dealer").

WHEREAS, Company desires to appoint Dealer as a non-exclusive dealer for the sale and distribution of certain of its products ("Products") on the terms and conditions set forth herein; and

WHEREAS, Dealer desires to be appointed as a non-exclusive dealer of the Products on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing premises and the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. Appointment.

Company hereby appoints Dealer, and Dealer hereby accepts such appointment, as a non-exclusive dealer for the sale and distribution of the Products within the following territory (the "Territory"): [Specify Territory (e.g., State of California) or leave blank for worldwide distribution].

2. Products.

Company agrees to sell and Dealer agrees to purchase the Products from Company in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in Company's price lists and other policies as may be amended from time to time (collectively, "Price Lists").

3. Orders and Payment.

  • Dealer shall submit orders for the Products to Company in a form acceptable to Company.
  • Payment terms for the Products shall be as set forth in the Price Lists or on invoices issued by Company.
  • Dealer shall be responsible for all applicable taxes and duties associated with the purchase of the Products.

4. Dealer Responsibilities.

  • Dealer shall use commercially reasonable efforts to promote and sell the Products within the Territory.
  • Dealer shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations in the sale and distribution of the Products.
  • Dealer shall maintain adequate inventory of the Products to meet the reasonable needs of its customers.
  • Dealer shall not sell the Products to any unauthorized third party.
  • Dealer shall not modify or alter the Products in any way without the prior written consent of Company.

5. Company Responsibilities.

  • Company shall provide Dealer with product information, marketing materials, and training as Company deems necessary.
  • Company shall use commercially reasonable efforts to fill Dealer's orders in a timely manner.

6. Term and Termination.

  • This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of one (1) year (the "Initial Term").
  • This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods (each, a "Renewal Term") unless either party gives written notice to the other party at least thirty (30) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.
  • This Agreement may be terminated by either party upon written notice to the other party for any material breach of this Agreement which is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice thereof.

7. Confidentiality.

Each party agrees to hold in confidence all of the other party's confidential information and not to disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party.

8. Limitation of Liability.

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER PARTY FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, ARISING OUT OF OR IN CONNECTION WITH THIS AGREEMENT, EVEN IF SUCH PARTY HAS BEEN ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.

9. Governing Law.

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [Your State].

10. Entire Agreement.

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous communications, representations, or agreements, whether oral or written

11. Severability.

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision shall be struck and the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

12. Waiver.

No waiver by either party of any breach of this Agreement shall be deemed a waiver of any subsequent breach.

13. Notices.

All notices and other communications hereunder shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been duly given when delivered personally, sent by certified or registered mail, return receipt requested, postage prepaid, or sent by overnight courier, addressed as follows:

If to Company:
[Your Company Name]
[Your Address]

If to Dealer:
[Dealer Name]
[Dealer Address]

14. Counterparts.

This Agreement may be executed in one or more counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
